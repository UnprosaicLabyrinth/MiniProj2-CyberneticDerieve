The Wheel


• A wheel is a circular component that is intended to rotate on an axle bearing

• A wheel reduces friction by facilitating motion by rolling together with the use of axles.

• A wheeled vehicle requires much less work to move than simply dragging the same weight.

• The wheel has also become a strong cultural and spiritual metaphor for a cycle or regular repetition.

• In modern usage, the 'invention of the wheel' can be considered as a symbol of one of the first technologies of early civilization, and thus be used as a benchmark to grade the level of societal progress.
